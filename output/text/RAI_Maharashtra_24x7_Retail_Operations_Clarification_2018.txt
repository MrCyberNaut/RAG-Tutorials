Subject:   Clarification required regarding 24/7 operation of retail shops 
and establishments in Maharashtra State. 
 
 
Hon'ble Principal Secretary, 
 
Greetings from Retailers Association of India (RAI)! 
 
Retailers Association of India (RAI) is the unified voice of Indian retailers, 
working with all the stakeholders for creating the right environment for the 
growth of the modern retail industry in India. RAI is the body that encourages, 
develops, facilitates and supports retailers to become modern and adopt best 
practices that will delight customers. RAI has a three charter aim of Retail 
Development, Facilitation and Propagation.  
We take this opportunity to congratulate the Government of Maharashtra to be 
the first state to adopt the Model Shops and Establishments Act. This move by 
the State Government has liberalised retail trade allowing retailers to operate 
24/7 in the state. RAI members have welcomed the initiative by the State 
Government. 
 
Sir, we are writing to seek clarity in regard to 24/7 Store opening permission 
for retailers in the State. 
 
As per the Maharashtra Shops & Establishment Act 2017, Opening and Closing 
Hours of Establishments (Chapter III point 11): “Notwithstanding anything 
contained in this Act, the State Government may, fix, by notification in the Official 
Gazette, in the public interest, such hours for opening and closing of different classes 
of establishments and for different premises, shopping complex or mall or for 
different area or areas and for different period.”

We understand that a notification No. MSA. 07/2016/C.R. 218/Lab. 10 has been 
issued to notify the working hours for certain class of establishments. This 
implies that all other Shops & Establishments are allowed to operate 24/7.  
However, our members have informed us that they are being approached by law 
enforcing authorities, who refuse to accept the new act, claiming that there is no 
particular provision which mentions that retailers are allowed to operate 24/7.  
We believe there is a strong need to issue a clarification by the State 
Government on the subject under consideration, thus mitigating the concerns 
of the retailers in the state. This would avoid complexities and enable retailers to 
opearate 24/7 without altercations with the law enforcing authorities
