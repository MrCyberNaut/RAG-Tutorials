Subject: To seek your intervention to provide relief to RAI 
members from generating multiple challans. 
Respected Sir, 
Greetings from Retailers Association of India! 
It is my pleasure to write to you on behalf of RAI. 
Retailers Association of India (RAI) is the unified voice of Indian 
retailers, working with all the stakeholders for creating the right 
environment for the growth of the modern retail industry in India. 
RAI is the body that encourages, develops, facilitates and supports 
retailers to become modern and adopt best practices that will delight 
customers. RAI has a three charter aim of Retail Development, 
Facilitation and Propagation and conducts various programs aimed 
at the betterment of employees in the sector.  
We are writing this letter to seek your intervention to provide relief 
to our members from generating multiple challans. 
We understand that ESI Authorities emphasize that the monthly 
payment of the ESI should be made by preparing/generating 
separate challans for each State and city. Upon implementation of 
the IT rollout, the ESIC authorities abandoned the system of 
payment of contribution through a single challan and have been 
insisting to prepare challans sub code wise to facilitate the region 
wise target of revenue and state wise counting of employees’.  
We are attaching herewith a letter in Annexure II of this 
representation issued by Shri A. P. Tripathi (Jt Director Revenue) to 
the Regional Directors of all RO, SRO and DO wherein sub codes and 
separate challan payment for each sub code was made mandatory 
and this circular bearing number P-12/11/11/103/2008-RevII dated 
30th October 2012 was completely contradictory to the declared 
policies of ESIC during the Diamond Jubilee Celebration.
