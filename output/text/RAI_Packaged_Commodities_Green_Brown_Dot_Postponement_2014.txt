Respected sir,
Greetingsfrom RetailersAssociationof India (RAI)I
It is my pleasureto write to you on behalf of RAI.
This is with reference to the notification issuedby the Ministry of ConsumerAffairs
dated 16th June,2014 with regardsto cosmetic products and toiletries bearing Red
or Brown dots for non-vegetarian and green dot for vegetarian products.
A few of our member retailers received notices from the Local Legal Metrology
departments for selling/ storing products in violation of the said Notification. You
will appreciatethe Notification hasraisedmore questionsthan it answered, namely:
1.
Retailers purchase goods for
sale from
manufacturers/
distributors/
importers whose primary duty isto complywith this new Rule.However,our
member-retailers are receiving regularly products that are violating the
Notification and its conditions since manufacturers' pipeline is planned
months in advance,like our members' purchases,hence such products will
flood the market for at least another 6 to 9 months.
2.
Manufacturers' labels are pre-designed, printed, and kept ready months in
advance. Therefore complying with this Notification means that such
inventory running into crores of rupees,should bewasted or destroyed. We
sincerelyhope that the intent of the Ministry in issuingthis Notification was
not to waste suchvaluable national resource.
