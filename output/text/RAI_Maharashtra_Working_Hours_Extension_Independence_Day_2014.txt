Subject: Seeking extension in working hours on 15th, 16th and 17th August 2014 
Dear Sir, 
Greetings from Retailers Association of India (RAI)! 
 
It is my pleasure to write to you from RAI. 
Retailers Association of India (RAI) is the unified voice of Indian retailers, working with all the 
stakeholders for creating the right environment for the growth of the modern retail industry in India. 
RAI has a three charter aim of Retail Development, Facilitation and Propagation and conducts various 
programs aimed at the betterment of employees in the sector.  
RAI is the representative body of Retailers in India having over 900 member establishments including 
large and small retailers in the country and represents over 98000 stores in India. RAI is the body that 
encourages, develops, facilitates and supports retailers to become modern and adopt best practices that 
will delight customers. 
 
Modern retailing caters to the changing lifestyle needs of the customer and is based on convenience to 
customers. You may be aware that end of season discount sales are very useful for consumers in India. 
Retailers in India attract a huge number of customers during the Independence Day week end.  
 
One worry that is synonymous to large number of customers coming to stores is that sale activities need 
to be wound up at normal shopping hours while the number of customers have not been catered to 
properly. Also if most customers come in from 5.30 to 8.30 PM, employees are put to pressure to deal 
with too many customers at the same time.  We believe that an extended shopping hour increased 
customer convenience and also reduces pressure on employees. 
 
Incidentally, this year, 15th August falls on a Friday and most of the retailers are offering Independence 
Weekend Sale on 15th, 16th and 17th August 2014. It would be a great help to the consumers , retailers 
and retail employees  if retailer stores can be allowed extended hours of working up till 12.00 mid 
night on the days of Independence Weekend sale i.e. 15th August, 16th August and 17th August 2014.
