An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Official Languages Act, 1964.
WHEREAS, it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Official
Languages Act, 1964, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby
enacted in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows :—
1. This Act may be called the Maharashtra Official Languages
(Amendment) Act, 2015.
2. After section 1 of the Maharashtra Official Languages Act, 1964
(hereinafter referred to as “the principal Act”) the following shall be
inserted, namely :—
“1A. The official language of the State of Maharashtra shall be
Marathi.”.
