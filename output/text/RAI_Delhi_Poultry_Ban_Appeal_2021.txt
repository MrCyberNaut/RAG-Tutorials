Subject:  RAI appeal regarding the order restricting entry/sale of dressed, processed 
and canned chicken and its products in Delhi, dated 11.01.2021 by the 
Government of NCT of Delhi. 
 
Hon’ble Secretary,     
 
Greetings from the Retailers Association of India (RAI)!   
 
At the outset, we appreciate the Centre Government for the proactive measures taken 
towards containment, control, and prevention of COVID-19 and continue to extend our 
continued support to the authorities in the ongoing fight. 
 
Sir, we are writing to you regarding the order issued by the Government of Delhi, on 
11.01.2021, restricting the entry/sale of dressed, processed and canned chicken and 
its products in Delhi. Subsequently, North Delhi & South Delhi Municipal Corporations 
have issued orders directing restaurants against serving poultry meat, poultry 
products or any egg-related dishes, failure to which action would be taken against the 
restaurants. 
 
Sir, while we understand that the State Government is concerned about the wellbeing of the 
citizens, we request you to consider that the decision to restrict the entry/sale of dressed, 
processed and canned chicken and its products and restricting serving poultry related food 
items will have a negative impact on the restaurant industry which is already struggling to 
revive from the aftermath of the pandemic.  
 
Sir, our members strictly adhere to the best food safety procedures and practices, 
including Sanitization, Screening, Social Distancing & Contactless service, through the 
handling & cooking of all products while following all protocols for the safety of the 
staff and the citizens. 
 
Furthermore, the World Health Organization (WHO) & the UN Food and Agriculture 
Organization (FAO) state that poultry cooked at or above 70 degrees is completely safe for 
consumption. Our member restaurants cook poultry beyond 100 degrees before it is served 
to the customers, which is safe for consumption.

dressed, processed, and canned poultry and egg products, when cooked beyond 70
