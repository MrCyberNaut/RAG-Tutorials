Hon’ble Principal Secretary,

Subject: Request for meeting in regard to Renewal of 365 days store opening 
permission notification in all municipal corporations in the state of 
West Bengal. 
Hon’ble Principal Secretary, 
  
Greetings from Retailers Association of India (RAI)! 
  
RAI is the unified voice of Indian retailers working with all the stakeholders for 
creating the right environment for the growth of the modern retail industry in 
India. RAI is the body that encourages, develops, facilitates and supports 
retailers to become modern and adopt best practices that will delight 
customers. RAI has a three charter aim of Retail Development, Facilitation and 
Propagation. RAI is the premier body of Retailers in India having more than 
1000 member establishments including large and small retailers in the country 
having approximately 5,00,000 stores. 
  
Sir, we gratefully acknowledge the move by your progressive department to 
allow all the retail establishment in the state of West Bengal to keep their 
stores open throughout the year. We are attaching the notification dated 22-
09-2016 issued by your honourable department. The said permission was 
issued for a year which has expired on 21-09-2017. 
  
The move has been much appreciated by the entire retail industry and this has 
helped the industry to provide an interrupted service to the consumers and 
also contributed to the increase in employment opportunities. 
  
We would be honored if the notification in regard to 365 days working of 
retail establishments in the state could be renewed. Subsequently, we would 
also request your good offices to issue a notification in line with the 
notification issued by the Delhi Government. Please find attached the 
notification issued by the Delhi government in this regard for your perusal.
